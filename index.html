EcoCircle

A sustainability-focused platform that combines AI-powered product scanning, waste tracking, and a green marketplace to make climate action accessible to everyone, especially people from lower-income and historically underserved communities. Enabling everyone to live and maintain eco-friendly lifestyles, regardless of their background.

Unmask

Unmask is a deepfake detection platform built to protect communities that are often misrepresented by existing tools and technologies. By focusing on fairness and diverse facial representation, Unmask identifies manipulated media that targets communities of color and empowers underserved communities to navigate the digital world safely, promoting accountability and trust online.

  • React
  • Python
  • TensorFlow / PyTorch
  • AI & ML

Curapal

Curapal is a health management app designed to support older adults in managing their daily well-being. It provides vital tools like medication reminders, vitals tracking, emergency alerts, and a caregiver community, making health management simpler, safer, and more connected. Restoring dignity and independence to older adults with technology that is uniquely tailored for them.

  • XCode
  • Swift
  • Healthcare
